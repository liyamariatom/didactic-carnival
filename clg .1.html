Welcome

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is a premier institution dedicated to providing top-notch education in the field of engineering and technology. The college is renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities, highly qualified faculty, and a strong emphasis on innovation and research. Located in a serene environment, it offers students a perfect blend of academic excellence and holistic development. The campus fosters creativity, discipline, and teamwork, shaping individuals into future leaders. With an array of co-curricular activities and strong industry tie-ups, the institution prepares students for global challenges. Amal Jyothi has consistently been a beacon of quality and integrity in technical education.

Library

The library at Amal Jyothi is a treasure trove of knowledge with an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It is a quiet haven for students to dive into their studies or conduct research. The library's digital section provides access to e-books and online journals, ensuring students have all the tools they need at their fingertips. Spacious reading rooms and a user-friendly catalog system make it a favorite spot for learning.

Canteen

The college canteen offers a variety of delicious and hygienic food options catering to different tastes. From quick snacks to wholesome meals, the canteen is a bustling spot for students to relax and rejuvenate. With its comfortable seating and vibrant ambiance, it is a popular hangout for discussions and socializing.

Sports Facilities

Amal Jyothi boasts excellent sports facilities, including a basketball court, football ground, and volleyball court. The basketball court is well-maintained and a hub for inter-college tournaments. The football ground, surrounded by greenery, provides a perfect setting for practice and matches. The campus promotes physical fitness and team spirit through its various sports activities.
